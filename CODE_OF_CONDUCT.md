# Code of Conduct

## Our Purpose

Civilization OS exists to preserve and make actionable the sum of human knowledge
for the worst-case scenario. Everyone contributing to that mission deserves a
respectful, inclusive environment.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful, constructive, and empathetic
- Welcome diverse perspectives — rebuild knowledge spans every culture and discipline
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users
- Prioritize accuracy and safety in all knowledge contributions

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Deliberate misinformation in survival/rebuild procedures
- Weaponization of knowledge intended for rebuilding
- Trolling, political derailing, or bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will address violations by:

1. **Warning** — Private message explaining the concern
2. **Temporary ban** — Removal from project spaces for a cooling period
3. **Permanent ban** — For repeated or severe violations

## Scope

This applies to all CivOS project spaces: GitHub, discussions, chat channels,
and any public representation of the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns to conduct@civOS.org. All reports will be reviewed and kept
confidential.
